Safety

Safety is one concern in this sport, as it is a full contact sport. There are limited rules when it comes to engagement of the opposing team.

The goal, being topple the pole by any means. Wrestling, punching kicking are all valid ways to keep the opposing team off your pole.

As this sport seems to come out of military training academies, it is no surprise that serious injuries could be a real possibility of you are not careful. Although not as popular worldwide as something like American football, safety seems to be the number one concern for the declining status of this sport in the recent decades.
