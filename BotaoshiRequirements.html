Requirements

Bo-Taoshi is a very accessible sport compared to most western sports like hockey and American football for example, as there are no limits on who can participate as long as you complete the month long pre-game training.

Not much equipment is needed for participation besides an open area and a pole, as even wearing any footwear is banned and team color jerseys are only required by the defense, the offense are allowed to play without any jersey and the helmets and shin pads are optional, although most participants choose to wear them and rightfully so.
